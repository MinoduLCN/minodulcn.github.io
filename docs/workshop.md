## Workshop Materials

### Atelier pratique 07/23
This first workshop was conducted on July 27 and 28, 2023, as a hands-on, station-based learning experience at the University of Kara, introducing participants to topics such as electronics, programming, environmental sensing, and climate adaptation.  
The full activity booklet, guiding participants through the workshop stations and exercises, is available here: ./assets/pdf/Cahier_d'activité_AtelierPratique.pdf
Publication: https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3757232.3757251

### Sound 
The workshop was conducted on March 2024 with students from the University of Kara. This workshop explored how weather sounds are associate with colors, emotions, and visual representations. The worksheet in English is available here: ./assets/pdf/EvenementsMeteoEN fin.pdf
Further activity using the workshop: https://futurium.de/open-lab-abend/open-lab-abend-klimawandel-auf-die-ohren
